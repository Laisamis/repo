Message from the Board of Governors

Board Chairman

"On behalf of the Board of Governors, I extend a warm welcome to Laisamis Technical Training Institute. Our commitment is to provide an environment that fosters innovation, practical skills, and holistic development. We believe in empowering our students to become valuable contributors to society and the economy. We are constantly striving to enhance our facilities and programs to meet the evolving demands of the job market and ensure our graduates are well-prepared for their chosen careers. Your success is our priority, and we are dedicated to supporting your journey at Laisamis TTI."

Message from the Chief Principal

Chief Principal

"It is with immense pleasure that I welcome you to Laisamis Technical Training Institute. As Chief Principal, I am proud to lead an institution that is dedicated to academic excellence, practical skills development, and fostering a vibrant learning community. Our goal is to empower every student with the knowledge and competencies needed to excel in their chosen fields and contribute meaningfully to national development. We encourage you to take full advantage of the resources and opportunities available here, and we look forward to witnessing your growth and success."
