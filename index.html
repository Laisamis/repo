About Our Institute

Laisamis Technical Training Institute is a leading mid-level college situated in the vibrant town of Laisamis, Marsabit County. We are committed to fostering a learning environment that equips students with practical skills, knowledge, and attitudes essential for personal growth and national development. Our programs are designed to meet the demands of various industries, ensuring our graduates are job-ready and competitive in the global market.

We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a curriculum that blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience. Join us to embark on a transformative educational journey.

Our Mission

To provide quality technical and vocational education and training that empowers individuals with relevant skills for self-reliance and national development.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in technical and vocational training, fostering innovation and producing globally competitive graduates.
